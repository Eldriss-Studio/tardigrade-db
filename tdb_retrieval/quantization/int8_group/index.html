Int8Group32 — plain group-of-32 INT8 quantization for retrieval.

Mirrors the Q4_0 group structure used by tdb-storage for archival (one f32 scale per 32 values) but with 8-bit values instead of 4-bit. Suitable for retrieval scoring as the inner quantizer; pair with a PerChannelScaled decorator to neutralize activation outliers before encoding.

§Why INT8 not Q4

Q4_0 has 16 levels per group (4-bit signed [-7, 7]). At Qwen3-style hidden-state magnitudes, the half-step is large enough to round non-outlier values to zero — destroying retrieval precision. INT8 has 256 levels (signed [-127, 127]); the half-step is 16× smaller, and even on outlier-heavy groups the non-outlier dims remain resolvable. Production retrieval systems (Faiss SQ8, Qdrant, Sentence-Transformers) standardize on INT8 as their precision floor for the same reason.

Plain INT8 group-of-32 quantizer. No outlier handling — see PerChannelScaled for the wrapper that pairs this with a calibrated σ to neutralize activation outliers.

Constants§

INT8_GROUP_SCALE_FLOOR
Floor applied to per-group abs_max to avoid divide-by-zero in all-zero groups. Mirrors PER_CHANNEL_SCALE_FLOOR but per-group rather than per-channel.
INT8_GROUP_SIZE
Group size for INT8 quantization. Matches the storage-layer Q4 group size for alignment.
INT8_LEVELS
8-bit signed integer range — half-width used when scaling abs_max into [-INT8_LEVELS, INT8_LEVELS] before clamping. 127 rather than 128 keeps quantization symmetric around zero.
